Our Story

The Chinese characters 妈妈 (māmā) translates to mom.

After the passing of Chef Lin's mother in 2015, Chef Lin decided to open his first restaurant in memory of his mother's cooking. He always envisioned opening his first restaurant in his hometown of Denver, Colorado and now this vision has become a reality.

Our Menu

Welcome to 妈妈, the destination for Sichuan cuisine and dim sum in Denver! Our chef's recreate unique and modern Sichuan dishes with the balanced use of fresh ingredients, herbs and spices. Be sure to try our famous homemade dumplings and hand-pulled noodles made daily when you visit!
